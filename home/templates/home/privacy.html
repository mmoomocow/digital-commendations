What data do we store?

We have to store some data to allow this website to work, what data is stored depends on if you are a teacher, parent/caregiver or a student.
Please note, this may change in the future as we add more features to the website, so please check back here.

For all users we store:

  • Your email address
  • Your login information (Username and password) for this site
  • Your name
  • Your role (Teacher, Parent/Caregiver or Student)
  • Your last login date

For teachers we store:

  • Your staff code
  • Your house group

For parents/caregivers we store:

  • Nothing yet!

For students we store:

  • Your student ID
  • Your house group
  • Your year level

When commendations are awarded we store:

  • The commendation type (One of the RISE values)
  • The reason for giving the commendation (optional)
  • The date the commendation was given
  • The student(s) who received the commendation
  • The teacher who gave the commendation

How do we store your data?

Data is stored locally in a database, this database is only accessible by select website administrators.
The database is stored on a server in New Zealand, and is backed up regularly.

How do we use your data?

We use your data to allow you to login to the website, and to allow you to access the features of the website.
We also use your data to allow us to contact you if there is an issue with the website.
Your personal information stored on this website is never shared with any third parties.

How do we protect your data?

All data is stored locally, and is only accessible by select website administrators.
Your password is encrypted using a method called hashing and salting, this cannot be reversed, even by the website administrators.

How long do we store your data?

We store your data for as long as you have an account on this website. It can be deleted at any time by a website administrator.

What are your rights?

You have the right to request a copy of the data we store about you, and you have the right to request that we delete your data.
You can request a copy of your data by emailing the website administrator.
You can request that we delete your data by emailing the website administrator.

How do I contact you?

You can contact us at any time using the contact us form. or by emailing the website administrator.
