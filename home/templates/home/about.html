This website is used to award and view Commendations for students of Bayfield High School. It a project being developed by Matthew Gray as a Computer Science task.

What are commendations and spirit awards?

Commendations are a way of recognizing students who have done something good. They are awarded by teachers and staff members and can be given to any student in the school. They can be awarded for any of our 4 RISE values: Respect, Integrity, Service and Excellence.

Spirit awards are a way of recognizing students who repeatedly follow our RISE value. Then number of commendations required for each spirit award is as follows:
Commendations RequiredSpirit award
25Dean's Certificate
50Green Spirit Badge
100Blue Spirit Badge
150Gold Spirit Badge

How do I use this website?

At this stage, only teachers and students can log in and award commendations. To do this, click Login in the upper right corner of the page and enter your username and password, or click "Login with microsoft" to use your school microsoft account Once you are logged in, you can award commendations by hovering over Teachers, then selecting Award Commendation.

At this stage, parents cannot log in, however this is a feature that is being worked on and will be available soon.

How do I get a login?

Teachers and students can log in using their school Microsoft account. To do this, click Login in the upper right corner of the page and click "Login with Microsoft".

If you are having trouble logging in, please contact Matthew Gray at 19059@bayfield-high.school.nz.

What data does this site store? Is it safe?

For information on what data this site stores, please see the Privacy Policy and security information.

How is this is a computer science project?

This website is being developed by Matthew Gray as a Computer Science project. It is being developed using the Django web framework, which is a Python web framework.
The source code for this website is available on GitHub.

You can also see the Inquiry, Report and Proposal that I wrote that lead to this project.
