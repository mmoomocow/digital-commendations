Welcome to Bayfield High School's online commendation system. This site allows teachers to award commendations to students, and will allow students and teachers to track progress toward school spirit badges.

This site is still in active development by Matthew Gray, a year 13 at Bayfield who is developing this for a scholarship project. Because it is still being developed, there may be unexpected errors and issues and if these occur please Contact us.

Login is now active for everyone!

Teachers and Students can now login using their school username and password, or using your microsoft account.

If you have any issues logging in, please Contact us.

For caregivers, you can now login to the site using your email address and the password that was sent to you. This will allow you to view your child's commendations and progress toward badges.
